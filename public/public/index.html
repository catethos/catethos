Reflect, Refine, Refactor

Fri, Jul 11, 2014 Reflect, Refine, Refactor Today I have read the book “Show Your Work” by Austin Kleon, the very same author who brought us “Steal Like an Artist”. Following the tradition of his previous book, the author serves us nuggets of advices, One of them that particularly resonates with me is: Become a documentarian of what you do. In the context of exposing yourself to the world, which is the theme of this book, the documentation of your working process serves 2 purposes: By far it is a superior way to showcase your work and your contributions than a traditional resume.
